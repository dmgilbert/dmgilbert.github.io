About Me

I'm currently studying civil engineering and mathematics at UNSW while also tutoring primary to highschool students in maths. I'd like the oppurtunity to tutor university civil engineering/maths/computing, but this is mostly out of pride because I reckon I can do a better job than some of the tutors I've had at uni so far...

When I can get past my laziness, I really enjoy making things and teaching myself new ideas at my own pace, not having to worry about submitting assignments on times or studying for tests. There's something almost addicting to the feeling of finally getting something complicated working, this is only amplified if what I have made is actually useful for something. It shocks me how I will willingly stay up late and lose track of time when working on my personal projects, but if I ever have to do any project/assignment for university it becomes a tedious chore.
